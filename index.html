Chapter I

The First Encounter

In the spring of 2025, amidst the blooming gardens of May, fate brought two souls together in a moment that would forever change the course of their lives. The air was filled with the sweet fragrance of roses, and the sun cast a golden glow upon the world, as if nature itself was celebrating this destined meeting.

She appeared like a vision from a dream, her grace and elegance captivating all who beheld her. Her eyes, deep and mysterious, held the wisdom of ages and the innocence of a child. Her smile, warm and genuine, could melt the coldest of hearts and brighten the darkest of days.

In that moment, time stood still, and the world faded away, leaving only two hearts beating as one. It was the beginning of a love story that would transcend time itself.

Chapter II

Growing Affection

As days turned into weeks and weeks into months, their bond grew stronger with each passing moment. They discovered in each other a kindred spirit, someone who understood their deepest thoughts and cherished their highest aspirations.

Her kindness knew no bounds, her compassion touched the lives of all around her, and her gentle spirit brought peace to troubled hearts. She was a beacon of light in a world often shrouded in darkness, a reminder of the beauty that exists in humanity.

Together they walked through gardens of blooming flowers, sat by the gentle streams, and shared dreams under the starlit sky. Each moment spent together was a treasure, a memory to be cherished for eternity.

Chapter III

A Promise

And so, with hearts full of love and minds filled with dreams of the future, a promise was made—a promise to wait, to grow, and to build a life together founded on love, respect, and unwavering devotion.

My dearest Rehab,

These few pages tell only the beginning of what I hope will be a long story. I will not say much now, for it is better that the days themselves write what is yet to come. I will ask for your hand after 40 months — because I want us to grow together and become stronger than when we began.

Until then, keep these pages as a keepsake of something beautiful that has just begun.
With all my love, Islam
